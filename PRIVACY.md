# Privacy Policy — Storyteller (Blood on the Clocktower Assistant)

**Effective Date:** 2026-04-20
**Contact:** syjia0325@gmail.com

## Summary

Storyteller is an offline iOS app that helps one player run
*Blood on the Clocktower* as Storyteller. **The app collects no
personal data, sends nothing to any server, and requires no
account.**

## Data We Collect

**None.** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal
information, usage data, device identifiers, location, crash
reports, or analytics.

## Data Stored on Your Device

All game state — player names you enter, role assignments, and
event logs — is stored locally on your device only, inside the
app's sandbox. It is deleted when you uninstall the app or tap
the restart button. It is never transmitted anywhere.

## Network Access

The app makes no network requests. It can be used entirely
offline.

## Third-Party Services

None. No analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting, no
telemetry.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from anyone,
including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the new version will be published at
this same URL with an updated effective date.

## Contact

Questions about this policy: syjia0325@gmail.com

# 隐私政策 — Storyteller(染布钟楼说书人助手)

**生效日期:** 2026-04-20
**联系方式:** syjia0325@gmail.com

## 概述

Storyteller 是一款离线 iOS 应用,帮助玩家主持《染布钟楼》
(*Blood on the Clocktower*)游戏。**本应用不收集任何个人数据,
不向任何服务器发送数据,也无需账号。**

## 我们收集的数据

**无。** 我们不收集、存储或传输任何个人信息、使用数据、
设备标识符、位置信息、崩溃报告或分析数据。

## 存储在您设备上的数据

所有游戏状态(您输入的玩家名称、角色分配、事件日志)仅
保存在您的设备本地,位于应用沙盒内。卸载应用或点击重置
按钮即删除。不会传输至任何位置。

## 网络访问

应用不发起任何网络请求,可完全离线使用。

## 第三方服务

无。无数据分析、无广告、无崩溃报告、无遥测。

## 儿童隐私

本应用不会有意收集任何人(包括 13 岁以下儿童)的信息。

## 政策变更

如本政策发生变更,新版本将以相同 URL 发布,并更新生效日期。
